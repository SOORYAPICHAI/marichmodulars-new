Award Winning Interior Design

Marich Modulars is an interior designing company committed to making the ordinary extraordinary. We are interior designers based out of Chennai. We are a comprehensive design company encompassing interior design, interior architecture, and known for creating beautifully crafted interiors. Our expertise ranges from full service in residential, office, hospitality and commercial interiors to first-rate product sourcing.

Our Architects and Interior Designers working have strong domain expertise in designing Corporate offices, Coworking spaces and their interior design to reach desired results. Our team is committed to delivering high-quality interior projects and innovative design solutions.We have a whole design crew equipped to handle any kind of architectural & interior design. As interior designers, we assist our clients with Interior Space Planning, Fixture & Finish Specification, Product Selection & Procurement, Artifacts & Interior Accessories Selection, Colour & Texture Consultation, Furniture & Fabric Selections, Custom Fabrications. We would be glad to offer you our expertise to eventuate your dream and give you the true value for your money by turning your space more functional with a beauteous and aesthetic look.
